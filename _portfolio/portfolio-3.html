" collection: portfolio --- Touchcord is an innovative redesign of Google's original I/O Beads project, which features a capacitive touch interface designed to enhance user interaction with technology. This project aims to create a seamless and intuitive experience by allowing users to control various applications through natural gestures. Touchcord detects a range of movements, including pinch, grab, twist, and slide, throughout its length. This multi-dimensional interaction model offers users the flexibility to engage with devices in a more expressive way, making technology more accessible and interactive. The project focuses on the integration of tactile feedback and gesture recognition, creating an immersive experience that bridges the gap between physical and digital interactions. By prioritizing user experience and ergonomics, Touchcord seeks to transform how individuals interact with their devices, paving the way for innovative applications in various fields, including gaming, education, and smart home technology.
